Campus Life







The playground at Dougherty is awesome and I remember playing football and basketball a bunch of times. I also remembered one time at my afterschool program, there was this decently thin wall that you could bounce alls off of. I remember me and my friends trying to flip water bottle onto the top of the wall, and right as playtime was about to end, I landed the bottle on the top of the wall. There is also another smaller playground, and an even smaller one.

My Favorite Subject!

My favorite subject through third and fourth grade was Writing. I especially liked it in third grade because I got to tell fun stories. In fourth grade, I didn't do good at first but I got better and eventually got A's! I also liked benchmark because of the stories we read.

That Concludes my opinion on James Dougherty Elementary, home of the Dolphins!
